## What is Crun?

**[Crun](https://crun.ai/zh)** is an AI-powered all-in-one content marketing platform designed for developers, indie hackers, and AI product teams. It integrates multiple AI models — including image generation, video creation, and long-form content — into a single workflow, enabling teams to produce and distribute high-quality marketing content at scale, without a dedicated marketing team.

Crun is built around one core belief: **every developer deserves a marketing engine as powerful as their product.**
